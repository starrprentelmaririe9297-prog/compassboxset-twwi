About.

We're all about creating good math and drawing tools. Compass sets are our thing – we focus on making them accurate, tough, and easy to use.

Our goal? To support students, teachers, and pros by giving them what they need to turn ideas into real stuff. Whether it's a kid learning shapes, a teacher showing how things work, or an engineer doing drawings, we want our tools to be reliable and help them feel sure about what they're doing.

🌱 How We Got Started

Basically, we wanted to make learning math and drawing simpler and more fun. It kicked off small, just giving basic tools to the local schools. Now we're known for good quality, doing new things, and caring about education.

From the start, we knew that the right tools matter in understanding math. A good compass or ruler can change everything. So, we wanted to make compass sets that help with schoolwork and get people excited about learning.

⚙️ How We Grew

Over time, we went from a little supplier to a maker and seller of full math sets. Now we got compasses for students, drawing sets for tech stuff, kits for pros, and see-through sets for exams.

We made our design and production better too. Now we do tools for all kinds of stuff, from school projects to building designs. Everything we make has to be accurate, new, and make people happy.

We always look at what's going on in schools to make sure our products fit with how they teach now and what people want.

🧮 What We Sell

Our compass sets are made for everyone – from kids to people who do tech stuff for a living.

Usually, each set has:

*   A compass that's easy to change to draw circles.
*   A divider to move sizes.
*   Two set squares for drawing common angles .
*   A protractor to measure angles.
*   A ruler to draw lines.
*   A pencil, sharpener, and eraser to keep things clean.

For people doing harder stuff, we also have sets with extra bars, leads, special pencils, and beam compasses.

We try to make sure everything is made of stuff that will last, like metal that won't rust and strong plastic. They're also made to feel good in your hand, so drawing is easy and fun.

🧑‍🏫 Why Education Matters

We think education is super important. Everyone should have good tools to help them get math.

Our team talks to teachers to get what schools need. Then we make tools that make teaching easier. We like helping schools and colleges by giving them tools that help students learn math well.

We also try to get kids into STEM (science, tech, engineering, and math) by making tools that get them curious and help them solve problems – things that are key in today's world.

🔧 How We Make Sure Things Are Good

Quality is a big deal for us. We check everything at all times - since the beginning until it is finished. Every tool is looked at to see if it is right, will last, is smooth, and safe before someone buys it.

We use new machines and good workers to make good products very consistently. We follow the rules and keep trying to make our products better so they last.

We also try to be nice to the world by not wasting things and by using stuff that can be recycled.

🌍 What We Want to Be

We want to be known all over as the best at making good, correct tools for school and tech. We want to join old-style learning with new things so everyone can do geometry and design, no matter who they are.

We want to keep growing by doing new stuff, being sustainable, and teaming up with teachers and groups who care about being good.

🎯 What We Try to Do

Here's our goal:

*   Make math tools that last, are right, and easy to use.
*   Make drawing and geometry simple for every student.
*   Keep our products safe, and made with good quality.

💼 Who We Are

We think it's important to be honest, do good work, and keep getting better. We work together and respect learning. Everyone on our team cares about education and making new stuff.

We also try to give back by helping schools, giving away learning stuff and helping students who need it.

🤝 What We Promise
